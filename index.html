About Us

Founded by a group of friends based in the United States, we strive to enact their original goal of bringing code to everyone and anyone, making it accessable, educative and fun. We want coding to be something people bond over, laugh about, and create meaning in their life. Through coding, we aspire to share the passion we at QuickScript share so ocean-deep.

Why QuickScript?

QuickScript stands on two proud pillars that distinguish it from most other coding sites.

01

Our Community

QuickScript has an awesome global community of more than 2 million interconnected users. We give feedback to each other, have fun coding together, and learn as a team. We are a friendly and welcoming community for everyone, no matter what background you come from.

02

Our AI, Codey

Codey is an advanced Natural Language Processing AI that helps every user learn in real-time when they program. Codey operates internally within the workspace, being able to learn from preexisting code and generate code to fit the current work within it. You'll never be stuck ever again.

We are both formal and informal.

QuickScript is used universally by both ameteurs and professionals, in both casual and serious settings. Codey is a great partner for beginners to learn coding, and excels at streamlining a professional's work.
